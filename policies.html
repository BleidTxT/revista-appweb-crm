Peer Review Process

All articles submitted to CRM Web Applications Journal undergo rigorous peer review. Each manuscript is evaluated by at least two independent experts in the field who are not part of the editorial board. The review focuses on originality, methodology, relevance, and contribution to knowledge.

Publication Ethics

Authors must adhere to ethical standards, including honest reporting of results, no plagiarism, proper citation, and transparency. The journal follows COPE guidelines in dealing with research misconduct and allegations.

Copyright and Licensing

Articles published in this journal are licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0). Authors retain copyright while granting the journal rights to publish and distribute.

Author Fees

This journal does not charge article processing fees (APCs). All publication fees are supported by institutional sponsorship.

Data Sharing & Research Integrity

Authors are encouraged to share data and ensure reproducibility. Conflicts of interest must be declared at submission.

Archiving and Access

All published content is archived electronically and available through our website with no access fees.
