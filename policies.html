Peer Review Process

All submissions undergo rigorous peer review by at least two independent reviewers. Reviewers evaluate originality, methodology, relevance, and contribution to knowledge.

Publication Ethics

Authors must adhere to ethical standards: honest reporting, no plagiarism, proper citation, and transparency. COPE guidelines are followed to handle any allegations of misconduct.

Copyright and Licensing

Articles are licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0). Authors retain copyright while granting rights to publish and distribute.

Author Fees

No article processing charges (APCs). Publication is supported by institutional sponsorship.

Data Sharing & Research Integrity

Authors are encouraged to provide access to data for reproducibility. Conflicts of interest must be declared at submission.

Archiving and Access

All published content is archived electronically and freely available via the website.
