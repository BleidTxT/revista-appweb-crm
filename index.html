Welcome to the CRM Web Applications Journal

The CRM Web Applications Journal is a scholarly platform dedicated to publishing research on web‑based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) applications, their development, implementation, and operational efficiency in service organizations, especially in gastronomy and hospitality.

Aims & Scope

This journal accepts original research articles, case studies, and reviews that investigate the design, deployment, and impact of CRM web applications and related digital tools on business processes, customer experience, operational efficiency, and organizational performance.
