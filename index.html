Welcome to the CRM Web Applications Journal

The CRM Web Applications Journal is a scholarly platform dedicated to publishing research on web‑based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) applications, focusing on their development, implementation, and operational efficiency in service organizations, particularly in gastronomy and hospitality.

Aims & Scope

The journal accepts original research articles, case studies, and systematic reviews that examine the design, deployment, and impact of web‑based CRM applications on business processes, customer experience, and operational efficiency. Articles highlighting the improvement of internal processes, reduction of operational times, and application of technology in service organizations are prioritized.
